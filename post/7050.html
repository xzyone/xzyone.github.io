McCarthy classified various marketing activities into marketing-mix tools of four broad kinds, which he called the four Ps of marketing: product, price, place, and promotion. The marketing variables under each P are shown in Figure above. Given the breadth, complexity, and richness of marketing, however—as exemplified by holistic marketing—clearly these four Ps are not the whole story anymore.

New Four Ps

modern marketing realities a more representative set that reflect the holistic marketing concept
People reflects, in part, internal marketing and the fact that employees are critical to marketing success.
Processes reflects all the creativity, discipline, and structure brought to marketing management.
Programs reflects all the firm’s consumer-directed activities.
It encompasses the old four Ps as well as a range of other marketing activities that might not fit as neatly into the old view of marketing.
Performance as in holistic marketing, to capture the range of possible outcome measures that have financial and nonfinancial implications (profitability as well as brand and customer equity), and implications beyond the company itself (social responsibility, legal, ethical, and community related).

Define: Marketing

Steps (Process) and Program taken to motivate or persuade (Promote to) potential consumers (People) to want your product, service, program (Products)

  • by satisfying needs and wants (therefore, has value) (or “creating” new needs)
  • such as Pricing and Place

Marketing Oriented

Understanding the consumer and providing a sports product that meets consumers’ needs:

  • Intelligence generation – analyzing and anticipating consumer demand, monitoring the external environment, and coordinating the data collected.
  • Intelligence dissemination – sharing the information
  • Responsiveness – acting on the information à designing new products and services and developing promotions that appeal to consumers.

Global Events

  • Broadcast to Global Audience
    • Significant Rights Fees Paid by Broadcasters
  • National Pride often a Consideration
  • Few Events in this Top Category
    • Olympic Games
    • FIFA World Cup

International Events

  • Appeal Transcends National Borders
  • Large Media Presence
  • Venue often Shifts Among Countries
    • League of Legends World Championship
    • Ryder Cup Golf Tournament
    • The Commonwealth Games

Nation Events

  • Large Portion of One or Two Countries
  • Often Large Media Presence
    • National Games of the People’s Republic of China
    • Stanley Cup Playoffs
    • CSL
    • The Ashes (England & Australia Cricket)

Regional Events

  • Little National or International Interest
  • Marketers Attempt to Move Event Up
    • Most Collegiate Sports Teams
    • (Most) Marathon Races

Local Events

  • Draw Fans from Small Geographic Area
  • Promote Using Local Media
    • Minor League Team
    • High School Sports
    • Local Amateur Competitions

Unorganized Sports

  • People Engaged in Sporting Activities Not Sanctioned
  • Controlled by Some External Authority
